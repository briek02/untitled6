Motion

Motion is the phenomenon in which an object changes its position over time. Motion is mathematically described in terms of displacement, distance, velocity, acceleration, speed, and time. There are 4 kinematics equations below that mentions these concepts: Displacement (D), Final Velocity (Vf), Initial Velocity (Vi), Acceleration (A), Time (T)

Kinematics Equations

Equation # Equation
1 vf = vi + at
2 d = 1/2(vf + vi)t
3 d = vit + 1/2at2
4 vf2 = vi2 + 2ad
