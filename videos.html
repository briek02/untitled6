Videos

On this page you can watch a couple of videos related to standing waves. Standing waves are where 2 oppositional constituent waves obey the superposition principle to produce a resultant wave that has either constructive or destructive interference. Sound waves are produced when holding a vibrating tuning fork down a pipe. The pipe is closed-ended, and the parts of the wave are re-mentioned in the second video.
